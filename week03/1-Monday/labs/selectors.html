    3. that Hungarian émigré Frank Varga followed in his father's footsteps to become a noted sculptor? ... that Singaporean authorities hope that the Mandai Wildlife Bridge will reduce the risk of animals such as Sunda pangolins and leopard cats becoming roadkill?

    4. that Arlington State College left the Texas A&M University System after 48 years to join the University of Texas System?

    5. that the annual film festival in Kigali is known as "Hillywood"? ... that William Jefferson Hardin was the first black member of the Wyoming House of Representatives?
